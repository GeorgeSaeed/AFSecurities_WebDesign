Mohamed Radwan
Chairman

Mohamed Radwan is AF-Securities Chairman, with more than 20 years of experience in the securities business and all market cap segments of equities markets.

Radwan's prosperous economic career along with his enlightened vision and leadership enabled him to have a significant role in the growth and transformation of AF-Securities; He has set a strategic plan and a culture for the company making a huge progress in the brokerage ranking since the acquisition in March 2018, as well as focusing on FinTech development in the capital market.

Throughout the years, He has always aimed to position his company among the top 5 houses; this was the case with Pharos Securities where he has spent 10 years and rose to be a Board Member after holding various positions including an Executive Director and Head of Equities, and was also the case with Delta Securities where he has spent 6 years being Head of Sales and Head of Institutional Sales. Prior to that he kicked off his career in Fleming-CIIC as part of the sales team.

Radwan holds a Bachelor degree of Arts in Political Science: International Relations from the American University in Cairo.
