Diviend Banks

  • All branches disburse cash dividends in local currency (EGP), as for Commercial International Bank – Egypt (CIB) branches they do both local and foreign currency (EGP & USD).
  • Credit Agricole Egypt only distribute USD dividends in Gomhoreya Branch.
  • Bank branches working hours begin from AM 8:30 to PM 2:00 , except for branches located in sports clubs their working hours start from AM 11:00 till PM 5:00.
