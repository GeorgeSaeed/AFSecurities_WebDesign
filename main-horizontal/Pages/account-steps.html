Account Opening Steps

  • Print Contract

    Print AF Securities Contract and bank custody contract.

  • Read & Fill

    Read the contracts & fill them out.

  • Bank Signature

    Sign your legal bank signature on each and every page of both contracts.

  • Valid National ID

    A valid copy of your national ID (Front & Back) if you are an Egyptian, or passport for non-Egyptian will be required.

  • Signature Verification

    Contracts will only be valid whether through signature verification, visit AF premises or signing in front of an AF employee.

  • Send Contracts & ID

    Send both contracts & your ID by courier to our mailing address: AF Securities, 52 El-Batal Ahmed Abd El-Aziz. 4th floor Dokki, Giza, Egypt.

  • Make A Deposit

    Deposit in any of our bank accounts List

  • You are ready to trade.
